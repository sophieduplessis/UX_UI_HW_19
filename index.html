About Me

Working in hatted fine dining for 10 years + and in the events and music production industry for nearly 20 I have acquired a very unique skill set. I believe everything is UX and its applications go beyond purely the digital realm. Empathy and understanding are my greatest tools when designing and solving human centric products and UI solutions. Psychology, assumptions from stakeholders and users inform initial design but my greatest passion is user testing. Research and user testing allow me to focus on the problem, if it exists and to create solutions the user actually needs.





Ux Skills

Research

Moderated face to face interviews are my favourite tools for research. I like go wide as possible and it brings me great joy analysing that data and the surprises you find.

Empathy

UI Design

I am firm believer in creating meaningful UI solutions the user actually needs while balancing stakeholders, never reinvent the wheel unless its backed by data.
